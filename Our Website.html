Our Story

We Started Where You Are — A Small Business With Big Dreams

P & S Projects was built from a simple belief: every local business deserves a professional online presence, regardless of budget.

Who We Are

Local Business, Local Understanding

P & S Projects was founded in Johannesburg by two developers who grew up watching local businesses struggle to compete online. We saw restaurants losing customers to flashy corporate chains, barbershops with no way to take bookings, and schools with outdated notice boards instead of websites.

So we decided to do something about it. We built a web studio dedicated entirely to helping small and medium-sized businesses across South Africa thrive online — with fast turnarounds, fair prices, and real results.

Today, we've helped over 50 businesses build their digital presence, from Soweto restaurants to Sandton barbershops, and we're just getting started.

What We Offer

Services Built for Local Business Success

No fluff, no unnecessary complexity. Just the web services your business actually needs, delivered fast and affordably.

🎨

Website Design

Your website is your digital shopfront. We create beautiful, on-brand designs that immediately tell your story and turn visitors into customers. Every design is custom-made — no templates, no cookie-cutter layouts.

  • Custom design tailored to your brand
  • Mobile-first, responsive layouts
  • Modern UI with smooth animations
  • Fast-loading, optimised images
  • Consistent color, typography, and branding
🚀 Delivered within 7 business days
💻

Website Development

We write clean, hand-coded HTML, CSS, and JavaScript so your website is fast, reliable, and works perfectly on all devices. No bloated page builders — just professional, efficient code that loads in seconds.

  • Hand-coded HTML/CSS/JavaScript
  • Cross-browser compatibility
  • Fast page load times (under 3 seconds)
  • Contact forms & interactive features
  • Google Maps & WhatsApp integration
⚡ Lightweight, no bloated builders
🏪

Business Websites

Whether you run a restaurant, barbershop, salon, school, or local store — we build websites specifically designed for your industry. We know what your customers are looking for and make sure they find it easily.

  • Restaurant: menu, gallery, booking form
  • Barbershop: services, prices, appointments
  • School: news, admissions, contact
  • Stores: product showcase & enquiries
  • Salons, gyms, and more
🏆 Industry-specific solutions
🔍

SEO Optimisation

A beautiful website is useless if no one can find it. We optimise your site so it ranks higher on Google when local customers search for businesses like yours. More visibility means more customers.

  • On-page SEO optimisation
  • Google Business Profile setup
  • Local SEO for Johannesburg & SA
  • Fast loading speeds (SEO ranking factor)
  • Meta titles, descriptions & structured data
📈 Get found on Google faster
🛠️

Website Maintenance & Support

Your website needs to stay fresh, secure, and working perfectly. We offer ongoing maintenance packages so you never have to worry about updates, bugs, or broken links. We're just a WhatsApp away.

  • Content updates & edits
  • Bug fixes and troubleshooting
  • Security monitoring
  • Performance optimisation
  • Monthly reports
  • WhatsApp support
📞 Ongoing support after launch
